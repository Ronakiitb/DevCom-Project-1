My Hobbies

Chess

Chess is a two-player strategy board game that dates back centuries, believed to have originated in India. Played on an 8x8 grid, each player starts with 16 pieces, including a king, queen, rooks, knights, bishops, and pawns. The objective is to checkmate the opponent's king, putting it in a position where it cannot escape capture. Chess involves intricate tactics, positional play, and strategic thinking, making it a game of skill and foresight. It is widely regarded as a test of mental prowess, fostering critical thinking, patience, and strategic planning.
